Digital Art

Digital art is another form of showing off a person's creativty, except you're doing it digitally.

The Artist

My name is Rhiannon Standard, a programmer who has a passion for drawing.

Best time to draw

In my humble opinion, the best time to draw is when the urge is there and the inspiration is knocking on your door. That is truly the peak time to vent out what you envision onto your art platform! Maybe you stumble across pinterest and see references that interest you, this can be done at any time when its convenient.

Best place to draw?

In your home, at a cafe, outside, any location that gives you the mood you need to properly apply your thoughts to your art program or piece of paper.

How to get started?

I first jumped into my art journey by watching various artist content creators on youtube. They teach you everything, mostly for free! It's up to you on whether you want to take a digital or traditional path.

Why digital art?

Drawing on a digital surface felt more convient and free-flow to me. I didn't have to keep going to michael's to get expensive art supplies to get the look I wanted. It gave me the space to keep trying without expense, that is, after you pay the price for your art medium. The ipad I currently use is a hefty $1000+ but there are definitely cheaper options ranging from $25+

Ai prompts

Below are the prompts I used with AI to help assist me with the hobby site.

  • The prompt used for JS code: "I need functional navbar links to hide the sections that are not active. Show me css or script that can properly hide and show them when clicked and explain how."
  • The prompt used for the hover effect: "Show me how I can make my navbar links more stylized, something fun and explain the code."
  • The prompt used for the icons: "Show me how I can properly separate my nav links in a creative way."
