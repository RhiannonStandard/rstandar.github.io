Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I specialize in portrait, event, and retouching photography services tailored to your needs.

How can I book a session?

You can book a session through the Contact page or by emailing me directly at contact@karlstarkeyphotography.com.

Do you offer retouching services separately?

Yes, I offer professional retouching services independently. Please visit the Retouching page for more details and sample work.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround time varies depending on the project scope, but typically, edited photos are delivered within 7-10 business days.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes, I am available for travel. Travel fees may apply depending on the location. Please contact me to discuss your specific needs.

Can I request specific edits or styles?

Absolutely! I encourage clients to share their vision and preferences. Collaboration ensures the final images align with your expectations.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made at least 48 hours in advance will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may be subject to a fee. Please refer to the Terms and Conditions for more information.
